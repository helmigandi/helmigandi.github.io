August 29, 2022

Download/Installation#

Yt-dlp github official.

  2. Download the video:

    yt-dlp -f [FORMAT CODE] [URL....]

  3. Download video with subtitle:

    yt-dlp --list-subs [URL....]

    Perintah untuk mengunduh:

    yt-dlp -f [FORMAT CODE] --write-sub/--write-auto-sub/--all-subs [URL....]

    Notes:

    • --write-sub: Download default subtitle;
    • --write-auto-sub: Download auto generated subtitle;
    • --all-subs: Download all subtitles;

  4. Download all videos in the playlist with index number:

    yt-dlp -o "%(playlist_index)s-%(title)s.%(ext)s" [URL Playlist]

  5. Download all videos in the playlist with index number and auto subtitles:

    yt-dlp --write-auto-sub -o "%(playlist_index)s-%(title)s.%(ext)s" [URL Playlist]
